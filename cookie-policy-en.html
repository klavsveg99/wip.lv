Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and improve your experience on our website.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Provide basic functionality like security and network management. Session
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with the website. Up to 2 years
Advertising May be used to show relevant ads (if implemented in the future). Up to 1 year

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies in your browser. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View all cookies
  • Delete individual cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies

Please note that if you block all cookies, some website features may not work properly.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these websites.

5. Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to analyze website usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about how you use our website.

Google Analytics is set up in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy, and we use Google Consent Mode to ensure GDPR compliance.

6. Google Consent Mode

We use Google Consent Mode, which allows us to customize Google services based on your consent. Cookie consent settings are stored on your device.

  • analytics_storage: Allows Google Analytics to collect data about website visits
  • ad_storage: Allows personalized advertising
  • ad_user_data: Allows data to be used for advertising purposes
  • ad_personalization: Allows personalized advertising

By default, all values are set to "denied" until you provide your consent.

7. Cookie Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy to comply with laws or changes in our practices. The latest version will always be available on our website.
